Game Modes

Every mode demands a different mindset. Know the objective before you drop in.

01

Team Deathmatch

First squad to the kill limit wins. No objectives, no mercy — just the frag count.

02

Search & Destroy

One life, one bomb site. Plant or defuse — every round is a coin flip on nerve.

03

Domination

Hold three flags across the map. Rotation discipline wins more than raw aim.

04

Hardpoint

The zone rotates every 60 seconds. Pack up, push, repeat.

05

Battle Royale

150 players, one map, one circle. Loot smart, rotate early, land last.

06

Hardcore

No HUD, no radar, low health. One clean shot ends it — respect the silence.
